# The Meaning Behind the looprig Logo

The looprig logo captures one simple idea: an infinite loop being squeezed into
a triangle.

The infinite loop represents machine intelligence. Its knowledge, reasoning,
and potential keep expanding. It has no fixed shape, no clear boundary, and no
natural stopping point.

The triangle represents the rig. A triangle is one of the simplest and strongest
structures. It gives the infinite loop a stable frame and turns unbounded
potential into something that can be directed and used.

The triangle does not try to remove the power of the loop. It applies structure
to that power. It makes the loop more reliable, keeps it within clear boundaries,
places it under human control, and makes its actions visible.

The loop still pushes beyond the triangle. This is intentional. Intelligence
will continue to grow, and no single system will contain all of its possibilities.
The rig must continue to adapt while preserving the structure needed to use that
intelligence safely.

The colors reinforce the same meaning:

- **Electric Lime (`#D4F84D`) represents the loop.** It carries the energy of
  high voltage, intense activity, and intelligence in motion. It is the moment
  potential becomes action.
- **Light Sky Blue (`#A2D2FF`) represents the rig.** It brings stability, trust,
  reliability, and calm professionalism. It is the structure that keeps all
  that energy dependable and under control.

Where the two colors meet, active intelligence meets reliable structure and
becomes a working system.

An infinite loop is potential.

A loop inside a rig becomes useful.
